The Reality

What does an admin week actually look like?

It's not overwhelming. It's structured. Here's what the commitment actually looks like.

Regular rhythm
📅
Monthly Admin Meeting

The whole team meets once a month to review what's working, plan what's next, and make decisions together. This is the heartbeat of how Nexus grows.

💬
Ongoing Discord Presence

Check the server regularly — not obsessively, but consistently. Respond to questions, keep an eye on the community, and flag anything that needs attention.

🤝
Teacher Touchpoints

When a Domain Leader submits a collaboration proposal, you liaise with a relevant teacher. This happens as-needed, not constantly — and you always have the proposal ready to hand over.

📋
Responding to Reports

When someone flags an issue, you handle it promptly. Most cases are minor and resolved quickly. Serious issues follow a clear escalation process — you're never left figuring it out alone.

🌟
Making Things Happen

When something exciting comes up — a cross-school event, a new domain proposal, a guest speaker idea — you're the one who turns it from a conversation into reality.

Why it's worth it
"You're not just managing a Discord. You're building something real — and the skills you develop here are ones most students don't get until university."
